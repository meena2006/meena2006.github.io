CRM BOT

Are you the person who just started a New Startup or a sales person who's messed up a huge number of customer data. Then this product is for you to ease some stress burst on your mind.

Customer Relationship Management software is a powerful tool to handle business and customer data to provide you more insights about your business.

Leads

All customers at some point were potential leads. A high potential lead can help your business to grow in the long run. Sales people need more organised data about leads to bring them on-board. CRM Bot can help you bring more information about leads and find the high potential leads for your business.

Customers

CRM Bot helps you to track the high volume of customers from various companies and Industries. This also helps you up-sell or cross-sell to customers from relevant customer data to grow your business.

Dashboard

Dashboard is a centralized hub where all data can be monitored in an interactive and dynamic way. It also helps you analyze and improve the business strategies for better business growth.

Projects

CRM Bot helps you track your project status like never before with right project members and cost-time estimation. It helps your customer to transparently have an overview in their project progress.

Task

High the number of tasks, the more it becomes complicated, but not anymore. CRM Bot helps you prioritize your task based on their billing, due date, dependency etc..,

Support

Loyal customers are created how fast you respond to their queries and support tickets. Support is an important pace which can throw you out of business or make you the market leader. CRM Bot helps you prioritize and assign tickets to right people, so that they can resolve faster and keep your business moving.
